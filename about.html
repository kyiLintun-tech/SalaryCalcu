Welcome to Empire Rubber

Producing the finest quality rubber in Myanmar.

Our Story

Empire Rubber began as a small plantation with a big dream: to transform the rubber industry in Myanmar. Through dedication and hard work, we've grown into a leading name, known for our superior products and sustainable practices. Our evolution from a humble farm to a state-of-the-art manufacturing powerhouse is a testament to our commitment to excellence.


Our Vision

To set the benchmark for premium quality rubber production in Myanmar, pioneering sustainable farming and innovative manufacturing practices.


Our Mission

We strive to produce the highest quality rubber using eco-friendly methods and cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to provide outstanding products to our customers while fostering economic growth and environmental sustainability in Myanmar.


What We Do

Rubber Plantation: Our farms are meticulously managed to cultivate top-grade rubber. We prioritize sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and ensure the health of our trees, producing high-quality latex that forms the foundation of our premium products.


Rubber Manufacturing: In our advanced manufacturing facilities, we transform raw latex into a variety of superior rubber products. We adhere to the highest standards of quality and efficiency, ensuring that each product meets rigorous industry standards.
